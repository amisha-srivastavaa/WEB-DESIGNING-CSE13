ABES Engineering College – History

ABESEC (ABES Engineering College) is a well-known private engineering college in Ghaziabad, India, established in 2000, affiliated with Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), and accredited by {NAAC 'A' Grade https://www.shiksha.com/college/abes-engineering-college-ghaziabad-24068}, focusing on outcome-based learning with strong placements in tech fields like B.Tech and MCA, offering holistic development, industry-academia ties, and boasting modern infrastructure. Key Aspects of ABES Engineering College (ABESEC): Establishment & Affiliation: Founded in 2000, it's an AICTE-approved institute affiliated with AKTU, Lucknow. Academics: Offers UG & PG programs (B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA) with a strong emphasis on technical skills, innovation, and research, particularly in Computer Science, IT, and Electronics. Accreditation & Rankings: NAAC 'A' Grade, NBA-accredited departments, and NIRF recognized. Placements: Known for good placement records with significant recruiters, high packages (including 59.91 LPA highest in 2024), and numerous successful alumni. Campus & Facilities: Features modern infrastructure, well-equipped labs, a spacious campus, and facilities supporting student activities like NCC. Culture: Promotes holistic development, leadership, and values-based education, aiming to create industry-ready professionals. Focus Areas: Strong industry-academia links, incubation for startups, and training in emerging tech like Cloud Computing.
